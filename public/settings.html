Instagram does not allow direct video upload from web apps. To share your reel, download the combined video and upload it manually.
How to share
  1. Generate and combine your videos on the main page
  2. Click Share to Instagram next to the combined video
  3. Download the video and copy the suggested caption
  4. Open Instagram and post as a Reel or Story
